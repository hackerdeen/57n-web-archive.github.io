Gooooooooooooood Morning music lovers!

Let's get some tuneshare on the go - I'm always looking for new tunes,
and always hearing new stuff.
My new excitement from the last week:

From the "Deep House with enough bass to ruin Tom's sub" department
comes Tiesto's Say Something:
https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=eDRj_h4TeFI

When it comes to a wee peak at about 3:15 it turns into this smooth,
chilled, sweet sounding cracker, shuffling along cheerily.

From the "6 minutes of Digital Pop Heaven" Department comes Porter
Robinson's album opener, Divinity. 

https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=si81bIoZRJQ

Total opposite of the Tiesto track - sharp synth sounds, strong,
digital, distorted beat throughout. Sweet, quiet breakdowns provide
a much needed contrast with the harshness of the chorus section. 
Loud, Lush and lovely - get listening. 

Finally from me, to confuse the issue further, comes Iceland's Ólafur
Arnalds. He's a multitalented multi instrumentalist, but the tunes I've
picked are quiet & chilled out, piano pieces with a string section.
He's got together a group of friends and filled his living room with
them, and recorded a few songs. The results are spectacularly relaxing
and provide a nice beacon of calm in a hectic day!

https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=wMSDPLOSHyQ&list=UU9XfDqYSm6ezuw0y_u3t_4A

That's all from me - reply back with what you've discovered in the last
7 days so I can get more new music!

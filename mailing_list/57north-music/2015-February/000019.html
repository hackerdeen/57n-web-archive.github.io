Musicmusicmusicmusic! It's the return of the wonderfully infrequent
music monday!

I found some potential freebies if you're interested!

Post rock act Lights & Motion has been on the go for a wee whiley,
previously named Aerials. 
It sounds a bit like Sigur Ros * Explosions in the sky * M83 with a bit
of Hans Zimmer, it's pretty great.

If you don't know post rock, it's kind of long, drawn out progressive
etherially ambient tracks that range from soft and twinkly to hard and
driving. It's wonderful working/waking up/relaxing/winding down music,
depending on album & artist.

Lights & Motion sounds like:

https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ZH8VGGvZBok from the new album,
Chronicle and
https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=lYfUNfK8tyQ from the first album,
Reanimation

His music's available from Deep Elm as 'pay what you want', so free ->
lots of money. See the 5 albums ordered oldest to newest:
http://deepelmdigital.com/album/reanimation
http://deepelmdigital.com/album/save-your-heart
http://deepelmdigital.com/album/unreleased-music-for-tv-film-vol-1
http://deepelmdigital.com/album/holiday-visions
http://deepelmdigital.com/album/chronicle

Go, listen, download and make your own mind up.
I love it, and everything will be on PiMPD tonight :).

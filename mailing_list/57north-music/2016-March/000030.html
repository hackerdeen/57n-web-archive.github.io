Howdy y'all

I'm coming back imminently and I'd like to do some alcoholing.

Breabach [1] are playing the Lemon Tree [2] on Saturday, touring their
new album.

Tickets are £13.20 + BF, and they're phenomenal live. Last time I saw
them it was a sit down gig in 2 parts with an interval.

This one should be cool as they've been experimenting with world folk
music this last few years - they had a big tie in for the commonwealth
games in Glasgow and an Aboriginal choir thing, so I think that'll be
making itself felt. 

My plan is Brewdog -> Breabach -> Somewhere else.

[1] http://breabach.com/

[2] http://www.aberdeenperformingarts.com/events/breabach-396140

Who's in?

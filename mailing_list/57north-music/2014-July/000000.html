Well there's happy new summertime electro coming from Electro Posé, our
collective favourite french electro merchants:

https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Py2GiQW6BfM

It's got that awesome vibe with what sounds like tasty marimba hits on
it. Get your ears round that.

If you don't know of Electro Posé, you've clearly not been around the
space this week and weren't at campGND. They cover a selection of really
hot sounds in electronic music at the moment - deep house, chillwave,
minimalist kinda stuff. It's all got a really summery vibe, so come
midwinter in Aberdeen we'll be sick of it. Have some examples of why you
should take note:

https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ACpOIX38Lvk http://youtu.be/cmSy_-TtvEc
https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=kEPgqvjwgtE http://youtu.be/6JT31Yll0r8

They also do groovy mixtapes that I'm really feeling at the moment -
listen to this one:
https://soundcloud.com/electropose/electropose-mixtape-teemid

And find all the rest on mediafire at:
http://www.mediafire.com/folder/23kdzqtu52dqk/Electro%20Pose%20mixtape

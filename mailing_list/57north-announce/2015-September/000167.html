Hello All

I will be hosting another film, on Thursday the film will start at 7.30

We will be showing another film from the CCC camp

I will be showing: * Crypto Dawn *
A To Do list for Cyptographic Research and Implementations

Crypto seems to be the last line of defence in a wired world. "We
mustimplement it, actively research it." (Eduard Snowden). So let us at
least discuss some To Do things. • How we can eliminate weak crypto
algorithms from the last millennium from our daily protocols? • How can
we deal with the standardisation mess in the field of elliptic curves
cryptography? • Which protocols and algorithms are needed in a post
quantum world? • How "opportunistic" should we encrypt the masses? Could
we provide a "moral, philosophical and technical commitment to enforce
and defend our liberties" Eduard Snowden has ask us for?

If you can't make it you will find the video here.
https://media.ccc.de/browse/conferences/camp2015/camp2015-6816-crypto_dawn.html

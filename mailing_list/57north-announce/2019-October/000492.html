There is just 1 week left until the Call For Papers for NorthernRST
closes. It will close next Friday, the 11th of October at 2200.

NorthernRST is a single day technology conference in the North East of
Scotland City of Aberdeen. We are looking for 45 minute talks related to
building your own tools or running your own infrastructure.

- Arts & Culture
- Ethics & Society
- Hardware & Making
- Security & Hacking

If you have written software to get stuff done or run a server instead
of buying it, then you probably have a story that we would love to here.

Talks should be aimed at a general audience (think experts in other fields,
enthusiasts with limited backgrounds or complete newcomers), but they should
also contain valuable content for your peers.

Details for the CFP and how to submit are on our website:
http://northernrst.com/#call_for_participation

If you have any questions about submission or NorthernRST feel free to send an
email to content at northernrst.com

- The NorthernRST Team

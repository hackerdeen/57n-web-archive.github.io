Hello Hackers,

Once again it is Tuesday, consulting the historic log 57North hold an open
night on this fortuitous day. Every Tuesday anyone is welcome to come a long to
the space and see what we get up to. Members will be working on projects,
reviewing the past weekends workshop, chewing the fat and other hackerspace
like activities.

Members should let everyone know what they are up to, for science!!

----------

Tonight is also CryptoNoise[1]. Every second Tuesday we visit a public place
and use Tor. This week will be our last in the Illicit Still, we are looking
for a new pub to use that matches our criteria[2]. We will be in the Illicit
Still from 1700-1900.

[1]:    http://cryptonoise.org
[2]:    1. Reasonable Wifi (not the cloud or similar, useful things not blocked)
        2. Wheelchair accessible
        3. Food beyond bar snacks
        4. Locally run (not a super chain)

Hi all

Please be advised that the 57N board are currently in discussion about the
correct course of action to take regarding Coronavirus in relation to
hackerspace attendance.

As of 2100hrs on Friday 13th March 2020, we are planning on keeping the
hackerspace open as standard but will monitor this as the situation
changes. We ask all members to be mindful of government guidance (
https://www.gov.uk/government/topical-events/coronavirus-covid-19-uk-government-response)
and make an informed choice about whether or not attending the space is
appropriate for you.

Keep well
The 57N Board of Directors

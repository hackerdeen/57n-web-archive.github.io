Hi all

I have been notified of a near-miss incident at the hackerspace regarding
an old (craft?) razerblade that was unexpectedly encountered under a pile
of soft materal. Hidden blades can bite hard and whilst I doubt (and hope)
there are not more of these about, it does hammer home the point that our
beloved space might be in-need of a big tidy, for member safety if for no
other reason.

I understand an effort to tidy the space followed the safety moment above,
but we certianly should make that a group effort. I know I still owe some
tidying up on the upper floor after wall painting and I think most folk
know about a mess they can help cure.

I'll be down Tues to do a big tidy and I invite you all to take part if you
can.  Depending on where you are tidying, armoured gloves may or may not be
needed; but probably wise to assume sharp things are lurking under innocent
items until the big tidy is complete.

Cheers,

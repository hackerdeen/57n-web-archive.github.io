Hey all,

It's time for the monthly Cryptonoise (in association with ORG Aberdeen
& FSFE Aberdeen) .

Come along to discuss digital rights issues, digital freedoms and
explore the use of cryptographic tools.

If you want to learn how to protect yourself and your rights in the
digital world,  come on down.
If you need to learn how to hide online, this is the night for you -
we'll be discussing darknets, interwebs, tor, i2p, hyperboria and online
anonymity with multiple identities.  

We'll also be on hand to talk GPG keys, signing & encrypting emails and
establishing your identity in the web of trust..

Bring along a laptop or smartphone and join us in browsing the web
anonymously, without tracking and discuss the reasons we need these
technologies and tools to protect ourselves. 

See you there!

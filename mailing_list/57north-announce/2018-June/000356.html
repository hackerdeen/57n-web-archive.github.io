Hello Everyone,


As some of you may be aware I have been working on Air quality monitors (Fine Particulate sensors) as part of project with a Torry community group and Robert Gordon University. You can find more information on the wiki [1] 

We have a workshop building the sensors and a talk about the project coming up in the next week. It is a good opportunity to advertise the hackerspace and it is happening in the library opposite the new space. 


Workshop - 23rd, June, 09.00 - Central Library, Aberdeen - more info on Facebook [2] and Eventbrite [3]
    Workshop to build 8 sensors to be fitted at participants house.

Talk - 27th June 18.30 - Central Library, Aberdeen - more info on  Facebook [4] and Eventbrite [5]
    About the Project, how it was formed and a wider discussion about air quality and further participation in the project.

Please share the links with anyone you think would be interested.

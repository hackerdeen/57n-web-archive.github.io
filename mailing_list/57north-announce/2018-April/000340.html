Scotland's first Hacker camp.                            `--|_,`'


The Hackerspace goes camping

campGND is a weekend long camping festival for hackers and makers running
June 1st, 2nd and 3rd 2018. 

campGND will be held in a scenic location in the North East of Scotland. With
power, wifi, weather and a connected field.

campGND is put on by members of the hackerspace, for most any neighbourly folk
that want to join us. We need your help to build out infrastructure to keep the
hacking going from a field.

If you would like to join us or help us connect our field please get in touch.

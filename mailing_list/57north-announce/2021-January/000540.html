It's Friday[1], and that means a contingent of us will be on Mumble from 8pm onwards. Come join!

To spice up the format, let's play some video games from 9 until close. That gives us time to have a drink, catch up and then we can focus on the logistics of falling out over video games. 

I propose last year's viral hit, Among Us [2]. The premise is simple, there are 10 crew stranded on a spaceship, but two in the crew are secretly murderous imposters! As a crewman, you go about your daily tasks to keep the ship running. As an imposter, you have to secretly kill crewmates without getting caught!
If a crewman finds, and reports, a dead body, we have a meeting to discuss what you saw, who you saw, and whodunnit. Here, imposters can try and manipulate us away from thinking it's them. Vote to eject one of your fellow players from the ship, and go back to tasking/killing. 

It's a simple game of both killing and lieing to your friends!

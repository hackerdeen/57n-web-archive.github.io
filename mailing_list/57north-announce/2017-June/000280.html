Cryptonoise is a monthly session in 57North run in partnership with the
Open Rights Group. 
It aims to bring together like minded people, facilitate discussion
about digital freedoms and to help people learn and explore the use of
cryptographic tools.

Usually we will discuss digital rights issues that are in the news.

If you are unsure, we'll happily help you learn how you can help to
protect your rights and your secrets in a hostile, digital world. 

Bring a smartphone or laptop and browse the web anonymously, learn about
these technologies and chat about the reasons we need them.

Tinfoil hats optional.

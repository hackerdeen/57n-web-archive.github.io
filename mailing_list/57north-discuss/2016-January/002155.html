> I am writing to enquire about whether or not you would be interested in 
> participating in TechFest 2016 Science festival.
> 
> TechFest is Aberdeen and North-East Scotland's annual festival of 
> Science, Technology, Engineering and Maths. (STEM) 2015 was the 
> festival's 22ndt Anniversary and is highly regarded as a key event 
> within education, industry and the wider community. TechFest aims to 
> promote engagement with STEM subjects for all ages through a variety of 
> accessible and entertaining programmes, composed of workshops, shows 
> and presentations.
> 
> The Primary Programme runs for a week and is open to all the primary 
> schools in Aberdeen and Aberdeenshire. It is held at the Gordon 
> Barracks in Aberdeen and the children come for a day, visiting 2 
> workshops and a show. It runs from 9am - 3.30 Mon - Fri, your workshop 
> should be suitable for up to 30 children and be in line with the 
> experiences and outcomes of the Curriculum for Excellence. The workshop 
> should last for either 45 or 60 minutes and suitable for P1 - P3, P4 - 
> P7 or P1 -P7.
> 
> The Activity Weekend is open to the general public and has drop in 
> style workshops held over the Saturday and Sunday  in the same 
> location.
> 
> The dates for this year are as follows:
> 
> Primary Programme Monday 29th  August - Friday 2nd September (set-up 
> Sunday 28th from 3pm)
> 
> Activity Weekend Saturday 3rd  and Sunday 4th September (set-up Friday 
> 2nd from 5pm)
> 
> If you could let me know as soon as possible if this is something you 
> would like to be involved with, that would be great. Our deadline for 
> confirmation is Friday 22nd January.
> 
> Alternatively we also run a Public Programme which is a series of 
> evening talks and presentations and if you feel that you would be 
> better suited to this then please let me know.
> 
> If you need any further details please get in touch.
> 
> I look forward to hearing from you soon.

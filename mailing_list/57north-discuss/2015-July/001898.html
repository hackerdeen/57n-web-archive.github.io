This picks on G+ and not twitter or facebook because in EFFs 2013 "Who
has your back?"scorecard thing[1] google+ didn't get a full set of
stars. But this is out of date. All three got a full set of stars in the
2014 report[2], and then EFF changed what the stars are for this year
and none of the three get a full set anymore[3]. 

I don't like the scary popup, and think the EFF scorecards over simplify
a complex issue (though they can be useful for starting a discussion, I
can't see a good way to have that discussion with people following links
on our site). I'd like to remove the popup from the G+ link but thought
I'd best ask before just doing it...

Hi,

Jim Killock came to speak at TechMeetup yesterday about the Open Rights
Group.

Open Rights Group campaigns on digital issues in the UK and are trying to
build up more of a presence in Scotland. They are campaigning for funds to
hire a Scottish member of staff. 

Please note this work is referendum neutral - if we vote Yes, a Scottish
group is definitely needed and if we vote No a Scottish presence is needed
to work with the devolved Scottish parliament and separate Scottish legal
system.

Hopefully you can donate and/or get involved!

https://www.openrightsgroup.org/join/found-org-scotland

For those of you that could not make it to TechMeetup, a video of Jim's talk
will become available soon.

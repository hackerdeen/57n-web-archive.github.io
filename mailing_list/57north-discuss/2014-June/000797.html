Dear all

Below is the text for TechFest workshop in September - can I get a show of
hands for who can make it?

*Description:*
Join 57north, an Aberdeen Hacklab, for a three hour introductory
electronics workshop and build a bendy, multi-coloured ‘MakeIt-Glo’ lamp
using the Arduino-compatible Digispark microcontroller. Use software to
programme its colours and brightness to complete the design - which is
yours to take home.

Been to Makeit-Glo before? Practice your soldering skills and customise
your Makeit-Glo, or put your 'programming hat' on and experiment further
with your own light presets.

*Booking details:*
Two identical workshops will be ran, the first from 10am to 1pm, then the
second from 2pm to 5pm. Under 18s need to be accompanied by an adult.

*Website*
http://57north.co/wiki/makeIt-glo

*Date/Time: *
Sunday 21st September 10am, repeated at 2pm

*Location:*
University of Aberdeen, Meston Room 311

Cool a few points,

If you don't want to include location and year of birth just leave it off.
The date format at the start of the line is mm/dd.
There should be a tab between the date and the text.
You need a closing angle bracket at the end of your email.

You can test that you added this correctly by running 
 $ calendar -t 08.12 -f calendar.57northmembers

That should tell you your birthday is coming up.

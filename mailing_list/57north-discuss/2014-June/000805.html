Hi,

We will be running an email self defence class as a "Themed Thursday" at
57North Hacklab on the 12th June. Themed Thursdays are open to non-members
of the hackerspace (although a donation is appreciated as we are using their
space).

You can find this on the 57North Hacklab calendar here:

  http://opentechcalendar.co.uk/event/1510-email-self-defence-tt

I will be giving a short introduction to PGP and encrypting email and then,
if you've bought your laptop along, you should be able to send and recieve
encrypted emails before the end of the night and understand what the
limitations of PGP are so you don't get caught out.

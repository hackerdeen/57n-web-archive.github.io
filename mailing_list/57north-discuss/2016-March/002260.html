*Communicaiton Security*
Saturday the 26th, 14.00, MacRobert building, Kings, Aberdeen University.
Exploring the current knowledge in communication security, and

Through APEG the Political Economy group I am doing a talk

*Technology vs Capitalism*
Wednesday 23rd, 16.00, Frazer Noble room FN115, Kings, Aberdeen University.

do this we need to have some understanding of the nature of them and how

prevent capitalism's predisposition to inequality.

Hi Folks

I'm doing a talk at AberdeenPHP user group this Wednesday at 6:30.  I kinda
mention this semi reluctantly as doing talks, terrifies the bejesus out of
me and I sort of think the less people go then better :)  but... putting
that aside, I think (hope) it'll be a generally useful and interesting
talk.

Talks at the PHP user group are usually quite technical and PHPey, but this
should be more useful for a more general audience I think.

Nominally it is an introduction to Joomla for developers, but it'll cover a
broad range of stuff.  Some questions that it might answer are...
- What is Joomla?
- How might I build a semi-complex online system?
- How would I get started creating a online system?
- What are the best options for Open Source systems for various application?
- How does Joomla work?
- What can it do for me?
- How do you develop with Joomla?
- What is the best way to do x?
- What Leonard Cohen song to people usually sing at joomla conferences at
the end of the night?
...so if any of those are kinda things you're wondering about, come along :)

It's a pretty casual wee group.

If you're coming, best to register at
http://www.eventbrite.com/e/aberdeen-php-tickets-20071197492 but really
just so Danny knows how much Beer and Pizza we'll need.

More info on the PHP user group is at http://aberdeenphp.co.uk/ and
https://twitter.com/aberdeenphp

More info about Joomla at https://www.joomla.org/about-joomla.html

Cheers
Andy

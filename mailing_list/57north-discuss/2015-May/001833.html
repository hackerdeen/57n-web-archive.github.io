Lightsail-1 [1] (A solar sail carrying cubesat) is going to spaaace at 
1445 UTC today!

It's piggybacking the mysterious now declassified X-37B shuttle mission. 
Get your tinfoil hats out - ~they~ claim they're not weaponising space 
and are doing science, but we all know not to trust ~them~. [2] 
~citrusfruits~

There's other cool stuff in that payload too - lots of research from the 
US Navy, including a TCP/IP server on a cubesat and more! Full manifest 
is at [3]

If you're interested, Lightsail-1 will have a 9600bd FSK telemetry 
downlink in the amateur 70cm allowance - there's a chance that we could 
contribute to realscience(tm) by listening and sending in what we hear - 
this may be worth looking into. Read more about the overview here [4] 
and in detail with timelines at [5].

What a time to be alive in!

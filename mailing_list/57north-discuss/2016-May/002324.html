Cryptonoise was rebooted last night, and I think we're off to a good
start. We had discussions about the Named Person Scheme, Investigatory
Powers, access to health records, Tails and Tor. We had a few Tails
stickers to give away, and there are a few remaining for the next meetup.

The future meetups will be scheduled for the last Thursday of every
month, so as to put a little more space between Cryptonoise and
TechMeetup. This makes the next meeting the 30th June.

You can RSVP at:

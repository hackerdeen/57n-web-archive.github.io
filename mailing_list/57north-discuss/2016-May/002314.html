Hello Hackers,

The Hackerspace is going camping, again. campGND is a weekend long camping
festival for hackers and makers run for and by members of 57NorthHacklab. Which
is now about two weeks away.

When:  20th, 21st, 22nd, arriving Friday afternoon, leaving on Sunday about noon
Where: Fish Farm on the Burn of Forgue, AB54 7XD 

On site there will be:
    - Pitches for tents
    - Power (maybe)
    - Internet connected Wifi (sometimes)

You will need:
    - A tent (your own, a space in someone elses or a place to sleep)
    - Entertainment (things to do for a day and a half)
    - Food, drink, entertainment
    - A head torch, torch, some sort of area light

We should be able to coordinate lifts out to the farm for those without cars,
the 57north-discuss list is the best place for this.

http://campgnd.com/

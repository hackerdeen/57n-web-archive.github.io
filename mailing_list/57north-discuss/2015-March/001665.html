Sounds a good meeting, nice one :)

Re an accountant...

I used to use Ian at TaxAssist on Rosemount Place, see
http://www.taxassist.co.uk/accountants/aberdeen .  He was always fair, open
minded, good at explaining options, helpful generally, and got everything
done.  I'm not sure if he'd be able to do it for £400 p/a but worth
asking.

Two years ago I switched to doing it myself using FreeAgent.  It is kind of
a nice system as it'll do all the book-keeping and imports Bank activity
automatically.  I think FreeAgent would be about £400 p/a.  I think it's
kind of nice if you're into doing things yourself and nice web UIs.

I'd recommend Ian at TaxAssist as a good option, but I'd also recommend
FreeAgent as an equally good alternate option.

So there are two suggestions :)

Cheers

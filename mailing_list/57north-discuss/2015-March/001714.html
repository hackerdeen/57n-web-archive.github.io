Hi All,

Just a reminder that the GPG event is tonight. Those of you that have been
paying attention will notice that I didn't send out a list of keys for you
to print. This is because I only recieved three responses and so it wouldn't
have been terribly useful.

We will instead be having a more informal keysigning and then we will be
looking at interesting ways of using GPG, poking at other crypto software
and looking at writing documentation for those that are new to GPG.

I will also be beginning the night with a quick talk to talk about some GPG
concepts to make them a bit clearer, as from speaking to people I don't
think these were really explained in the documentation they've read.

just print out some strips with your key fingerprint on them and bring them
along to hand out.

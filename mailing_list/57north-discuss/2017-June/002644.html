Subject: IET open-door day

Good afternoon,

Aberdeen Science centre is hosting the 2017 IET (Institute of Engineering and Technology) open-doors day in collaboration with Shell. We are looking to showcase the amazing range of engineering in the North-East.

We would like to invite 57 North Hacklab to come along to our public event on 29th July 2017 and run an interactive booth/stall. I visited your stall at the Universities May Festival - 'Hall of Mayhem', and I think you would add a very popular different angle into engineering. There are not strict guidelines, but we are looking for an interactive and engaging, engineering themed activity. The event will run from 10 am - 4pm with setup from 8.30am. Lunch will be provided and power and Wi-Fi can be supplied if required.

Please get in touch if you have any questions, and if you are interested, what you would like to bring along.

Kind regards

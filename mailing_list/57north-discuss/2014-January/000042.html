Hi all,

There are two events you should sign up for if you want to go into the games industry.

First, get down to Dundee on 1 March
http://scottishgames.net/2014/01/28/game-in-scotland-2014/
sign up is free and this is where you look for jobs and speak to devs and founders

Second, go north to Elgin for 14-16 March for the Moray Games Jam organised by their local games industry
http://scottishgames.net/2014/01/30/moray-games-jam-be-there/
sort out a team and be ready to apply by 14 February

Hi All,

The van will be packed for CCC camp during the day on the 17th August.
This means all items SHOULD be already upstairs on the evening of the
16th to allow us to pack the van in the best way possible.

If this is not possible, please advise that you'll be bringing things
late so we can account for this in the van packing.

See https://scottishconsulate.org/ccc2019/overview#scotconvoy for more
details.

On the evening of the 17th we will be participating in #WeAreNameless,
announcement email to follow shortly.

The van will be leaving early on the 18th. There will be no packing on
the 18th.

Please do not have anything that is covered by export restrictions, or
prohibited from transport by sea.

Thanks,
Iain.

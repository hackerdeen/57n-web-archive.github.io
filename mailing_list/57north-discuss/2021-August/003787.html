As part of the current exhibition about Aberdeen People's Press, Peacock Visual Arts are inviting all you lovely people to come and work with them to
create posters for your campaigns. ps - it is free!

Use a combination of Risograph printer & Letterpress to create visually striking posters.
 
From now until 25th September and by appointment only: Thursday - Saturday
 
Within the context of Another World is Possible, Peacock Visual Arts would like to invite the city’s local activists and campaigners to produce
posters. You can produce up to ten posters free of charge, in relation to your community’s campaign. We can accommodate groups of up to 6 people. For
more details and for booking please email [1]neil at peacock.studio

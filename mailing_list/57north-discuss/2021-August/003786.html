Subject: Aberdeen People's Press exhibition and programme of talks

Think nothing ever happened in the Granite City! Think Again!


There is a fantastic exhibition on just now at the Peacock Visual Arts space (the worm), about Aberdeen's People's Press, which was a socialist
printer and publisher in the city in the 1970s/1980s. The exhibition has been created by former members of the Press.

 
For more information see here - 
[1]https://worm.gallery/showing/another-world-is-possible
[2]https://www.facebook.com/peacockvisualarts/
Press Pack can be downloaded here - [3]https://we.tl/t-23hnjwzU9J
 
There are also 5 talks accompanying the exhibition and they will feature former members of the Press, as well some special guests from Aberdeen
Social Centre! The 1st talk is on Sunday (1st August). They are Zoom webinars but the (free) tickets are bookable through Eventbrite. All of them are
here - 

[4]https://www.eventbrite.co.uk/o/peacock-visual-arts-17459321692

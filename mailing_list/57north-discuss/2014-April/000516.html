SpaceX and Nasa have scheduled another attempt to launch CRS-3 on
Monday evening. With luck the launch will proceed this time; carrying
HackSat One into orbit.

The launch is scheduled for 20:48 UTC on Monday 14th April.
Coverage starts at 20:00 UTC (that's 9pm London time).

Come along to the London Hackspace on Monday evening to watch the

try to have a dev board and antenna on hand to show the satellite in
action.

http://www.spacex.com/webcast/

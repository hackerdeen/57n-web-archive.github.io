I got a little sidetracked this evening and doorbot now has some support
for playing cards against hackspace. It's statements and answers are
based off http://piratepad.net/FPz65q7zeW

doorbot.57north.co/cah gives you the result of doorbot playing on it's
own
doorbot.57north.co/cah_say does the same but doorbot also says the piece
of wisdom it has created to annoy or enlighten anyone in the space. 
doorbot.57north.co/cah.json gives a piece of wisdom in a JSON format for
ease of integrating it into other things. 

To use this with an actual cards against humanity game I was thinking
get one card from doorbot's set to add to your hand from the normal deck
so doorbot.57north.co/cah_answer gives one virtual answer card. 

If you want to play with statements from doorbot's set:
doorbot.57north.co/cah_statement

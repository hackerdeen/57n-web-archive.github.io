> But I will go read etiquette as im not sure that everyone wants all the

The problem was not the initial message, that is exactly the sort of
thing that should be going on the announcement list. A notification of
an upcoming event.

The discussion that followed should have been on the -discuss list
however.

If people wanted to not be subscribed to -discuss but still wanted to
see the replies, they can see the -discuss list via NNTP or via the
online web interfaces at:

 * http://news.gmane.org/gmane.org.hackerspaces.57north
 * http://blog.gmane.org/gmane.org.hackerspaces.57north
 * http://lists.57north.co/pipermail/57north-discuss/

The -announce list is intended to be _low volume_. That means no more
than one email a day, and no more than three a week, as a general rule
(although there will be busy times and less busy times).

Hi Mojca,

I ran the Tor talk & would be happy to go over things, though this week is
rather busy, as I'm running a Locksport / lock picking workshop on Thursday
evening (feel free to pop in if interested)

As regards your accounts, it is possible that some may have locked as a
"security feature" as they noticed logins from outside your normal
geographic location - this is because the Tor exit node could be anywhere
in the world & a far as yahoo / eBay  is concerned, your access is coming
from the exit node - Im assuming they have (rightly) assumed you're not in
that location & may have warned / emailed you as a result.  There may be
options to disable the warnings / set certain behaviour limits with your
accounts, however I will be the first to say I haven't used either of those
services over Tor myself.

Beyond that, I'm happy to say that I'm not a Tor expert, but I am
interested in learning more myself & would be happy to sit down, go over
the issues you've experienced & see if we can research / identify a cause
together.

Regarding the other machines etc, would be best to get details of the
machines in question, but in principal anything that is working & not
really old is likely to be of use.

However (personal opinion, others may disagree) I would usually recommend
against donations that are known to be faulty, unless a specific member has
a project in mind or is volunteering to assess / fix the machines in
question (feel free to volunteer folks!) as otherwise it is likely to
remain unfixed, as many of us are already juggling many exuding projects!

Regards
Tony

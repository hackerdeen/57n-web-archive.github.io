where we discuss important space matters, so it may not be very

> My aim is to first map out, then grow the Open Data ecosystem in Aberdeen through engagement, events, data releases and cross-working between institutions, informal groups and other bodies. We would be interested in producing data as well as consuming it. I have

electronics experience. If the city council would like to give us an
endless supply of money, we could hack Aberdeen into the smartest
bankrupt city in the world.

 Robert Gordon UniversityStudio ManagerJob descriptionMAKE is a fab-lab that is being delivered by RGU in the centre of Aberdeen, through a partnership agreement with Aberdeen City Council. The facility has existing and growing high-tech manufacturing capacity and we are seeking an experienced individual to deliver and develop this important and exciting resource. 

In this role you’ll be responsible for the day to day management of the MAKE facility. You’ll provide technical support as well as acting as the public 'face' of the MAKE facility. Additionally you’ll be the link between stakeholders and the University. You’ll already hold a degree in a Design/ Architecture/ Engineering discipline and will have previous experience of delivering excellent customer service, as well as CAD design and design thinking. 

Working hours will be 37 hours per week and this will include Saturdays 10am - 5pm and Wednesdays 1pm - 8pm. 

There are a restricted number of certificates of sponsorship available in the UK and there is no guarantee that the University would be able to obtain one should the successful candidate require one.

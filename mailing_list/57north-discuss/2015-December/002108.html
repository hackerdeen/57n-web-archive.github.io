Apply NOW to join the Edinburgh Mini Maker Faire, Sunday 10 April 2016.
[cid:image001.jpg at 01D138E4.0CB80320]

Do you love to make stuff?

Have you made your own gizmos, musical instruments, quadcopters, submarines or rockets?

Do you tinker with toys, robots, toy-robots or robot-toys?

The Edinburgh Mini Maker Faire is your chance to showcase your inventions, crafts, and tools with other makers and the general public!

Now in its fourth year, the Edinburgh Mini Maker Faire is a celebration of human creativity, bringing together all sorts of makers from across the UK and beyond under one big roof.

Apply NOW: makerfaireedinburgh.com<http://makerfaireedinburgh.com/>

Applications close Friday 15 January 2016.

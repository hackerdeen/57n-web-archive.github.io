> I need help with HTML auto-refresh:

> I want a page that does auto-referesh to update a page that doesn't 
> every 15s:
> [...]

Andy and Peter suggested I use "iframe" - This example is based on the 
example Andy sent me, modified to fill the viewport:

<head>
   <title>Tony's fresh page</title>
   <meta http-equiv="refresh" content="15">
</head>
<body>
   <iframe 
src="http://futaba:9425/mfs.cgi?MOrev=0&HDaddrname=1&MOorder=150&CCdata=192.168.0.248%3A9422&HDorder=3&HDrev=1&sections=HD" 
width="100%" height="100%">
   </iframe>
</body>
</html>

Works a treat :-)

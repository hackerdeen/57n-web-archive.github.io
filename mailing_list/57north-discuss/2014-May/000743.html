Tech

Linux Outlaws - The best linux podcast
linuxoutlaws.com

The Big Web show - Jeffery Zeldmans podcast, web shit
5by5.tv/bigwebshow

MacBreak Weekly - Current affairs in the mac world 
twit.tv/mbw

Triangulation - Interviews with tech people
twit.tv/tri

This Week in Google - Current affairs in google and cloud 
twit.tv/twig

TWiT - This week in tech current affairs 
twit.tv/twit

Gweek - Podcast by Mark Frauenfelder, editor of Make and BoingBoing
gweek.net

Accidental Tech Podcast - General Tech podcast by three developers
atp.fm

Creative Coding Podcast - Podcast of visual artist seb.ly
creativecodingpodcast.com

Debug - Interviews with developers with interesting pasts 
imore.com/debug

Iterate - App Design/Developer interview
imore.com/debug

BSDtalk - BSD related interviews
bsdtalk.blogspot.co.uk/

BSDNow - BSD Magazine show
bsdnow.tv


Games

84play - Podcast by translation company 8-4 play
8-4.jp

Expericence Points Podcast
experiencepoints.net

The Brainy Gamer -  Podcast about issues in games and gaming
brainygamer.com

Double Fine Action Cast - Double Fines podcast
doublefine.com/dfactioncast

DF Talk	- Dwarf Fortress Podcast
bay12games.com/dwarves/df_talk.html


Science Fiction

Cory Doctorow - Cory's podcast blog, updates, articles and stories read by Cory.
Craphound.com

Beware the Hairy Mango - Bizare Flash fiction
bewarethehairymango.com

Starship Sofa -	Hugo Award winning Scifi Magazine podcast.
starshipsofa.com

Machine of Death - Stories about the Machine of Death
machineofdeath.net/about/podcast

They are the podcasts in Pocket that I can remember being any good. Quite a few
aren't updated that often, but others are weekly. A lot of them are 2 hours or
so.

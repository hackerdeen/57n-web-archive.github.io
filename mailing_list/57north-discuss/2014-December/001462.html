Good morning hackers of Aberdeen!

Today's Monday - the exciting beginning of a new week that carries with
it opportunity, excitement and the thrill of the final run up to the
nonspecific Winter festivities!

Have you bought the presents you're supposed to? Have you organised
travel plans? Have you thought about what you're going to be drinking on
the *big day(s)*?
Nope, neither have I!

As an alternative, let me recommend that you come along to the
hackerspace this fine Monday eve and play with electronics in the aptly
name Aberdeen Electronics night, ABZ.Electron. 
Escape the Shopping Rush, hide from the hordes of people looking to
further the consumerist agenda or just come along and make your own non
denominational winter holiday themed lighting.

I'll be there from 8ish, prodding at cameras, circuits and maybe I'll
finally get to doing KiCAD like I've been saying for weeks now.

As we said in back int'day in Ancient Rome, Io Saturnalia!

Hi all,

Some of us are cyclists in the space, and there's interesting cycle things beginning to happen in the city. After the weekend's protest on Broad St[1] there seems to be a bit of movement!

There's an event being held at RGU on 26th September at 1915 - details are at [2] to look at beginning to form a unified Grampian region cycling advocacy group, to capture and promote the interests of all cyclists in the area - not just the lycra clad loons like me, but parents and children, mountain bikers and more.

If you're interested in cycling, or are invested in improving the city's infrastructure and accessibility, this is probably an event worth going to!

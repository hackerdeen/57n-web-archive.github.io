Hi all,


 


Corinna Bruce from FanDuel is speaking about developing a site for the
Edinburgh Dog and Cat Home (EDCH). 


FanDuel encourage both charity initiatives and engineers also get 10%
time to develop themselves or something of interest.  Corinna was
involved in a small team that created an app for EDCH and will tell us
through how she worked with EDCH to define the requirements, the tools
that were used to create the app, challenges encountered, the release of
the app and feedback from customers. 


https://www.facebook.com/events/293937787765612/

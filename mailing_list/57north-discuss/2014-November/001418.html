Hello Hackers,

It is time again for another issue of the 57North Newsletter. Without your help

newsletter. We want to know about interesting things that have happened in the

The deadline for articles is the 10th of December 2014. Articles should be sent
to myself (jones at sdf.org) in a plain text format, ASCII art is encouraged.

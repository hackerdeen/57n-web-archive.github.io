> Hi, Robert.
>
> I think there are two quite different cases:
>
> 1. Member uses the space, but doesn't pay subs.
> 2. Member doesn't use the space and doesn't pay subs.
>
> Case #1 automatic dismemberment prevents free-loaders using the space.
> Case #2 automatic dismemberment helps maintain space security.
>
> I favour automatic dismemberment according to clear rules. However,
> asking someone who hasn't actually been using the space to pay the subs.
> that they owe retrospectively is a disincentive to continuing their
> membership. I think it would be better to follow the new member
> procedure to reinstate anyone who has been automatically dismembered.

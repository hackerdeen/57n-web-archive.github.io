> Let's try workadventure![1] 
> Some may be familiar with this as the way "RC3" ran this year.
> 
> It's a little 16 bit browser based adventure with inbuilt Jitsi! 
> Hopefully this'll make audio setup/etc easier.
> I've spent some time fiddling with irl's already excellent map - we 
> have two rooms to occupy and some toys to play with.
> 
> Each of the rooms has a Jitsi meeting and standing in the marked areas 
> (there are a few, if they're in the same room they're linked) will join 
> you into that room's Jitsi. 
> If you stand close to someone or a couple of folks outside the marked 
> areas, it'll spawn a separate call with you both in it, so you can 
> wander away from the rest of the group for a chat. 
> If anyone's really struggling, there are also direct Jitsi links to 
> each room that I can give out.

Hi,

Last night I announced a new wiki for 57North. Our existing wiki has grown
stale and has a lot of old, irrelevant and, in many cases, incorrect
information on it.

You can find the new Wiki at https://wiki.57north.org.uk/. (You can find the
CAcert root certificates at https://www.cacert.org/index.php?id=3 or, if you
prefer to have an unencrypted connection, you can use http://).

In many places throughout the wiki now, information on pages is shown in
summary form on other pages, creating a richer experience when browsing the
wiki and meaning that there is less duplication of information on the wiki.

On the 17th January, there will be a "Wiki Hack Day" at the space where we
will look at migrating any useful content from the old wiki and documenting
things about the space on the new wiki. I will also be explaining the
concepts of the new semantic wiki and how the classes, properties, templates
and forms work together. This event will start at 1pm although I will
probably be in earlier eating some lunch.

If you have any immediate questions, please reply on the mailing list,
otherwise I hope to see you at the space on the 17th.

Thanks for your attention,
Iain.

On Tue, Jan 13, 2015 at 11:06:45AM +0000, Tom Jones wrote:
> I have always felt uncomfortable with the camera being on all the time in the
> space. Coming from CCC and Germany I think we are too forgiving with constant
> surveillance in this country.

I agree that the webcam should be removed. The novelty of the webcam has
worn off and it is now just creepy.

One of our aims as a hackerspace is to provide a safe space to work on
projects. I believe that a webcam allowing anyone to view the space breaches
this safe space and is therefore contrary to that goal.

I believe this to be a strong enough reason to remove the webcam from the
hackerspace. If anyone has a reason that they would like to keep the webcam
in the space, this should be discussed here so that we can reach an
arrangement that works for everyone.

Thanks,
Iain.

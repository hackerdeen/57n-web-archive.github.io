Transform Digital: Aberdeen 4th February 2015, AECC, Aberdeen
With construction of the Aberdeen's new digital network due to begin next
month, key stakeholders are uniting to discuss the impact of the *changing
digital landscape* on the city and shire's business community.

At *Transform Digital: Aberdeen*, representatives from CityFibre and IFB
will be joined by UK and Scottish Government, Scottish Enterprise and local
councils to lead discussions on Aberdeen's digital future and its impact on
the local economy.

Come and find out:

   - What becoming a *Gigabit City* means for Aberdeen and the regional
   economy
   - How this new infrastructure can benefit your business
   - The impact on innovation and entrepreneurial activity in Aberdeen and
   beyond
   - How small businesses can get a free connection using a government grant

 The conference will be a high impact event. As with all INCA events
networking and learning opportunities will be maximised.

The conference will be accompanied by an exhibition showcasing some of the
most innovative, can-do companies and projects in the industry.

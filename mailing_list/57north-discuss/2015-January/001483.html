Afternoon hackers!

Just an update to say I'm reducing the frequency of my Monday Night
electronics centered session in, case others want to do different things.
Plus, I'd like some free time. This night will be resuming on Monday 12th
January 

I'm also introducing two new, less regular nights: OSM night on the 3rd
Thursday of every second month, starting on Thursday 15th January, and
then happening again on the 19th of March. This will give us a chance to
discuss the state of the map in Aberdeen, new toys and tricks we've been
using. If new faces want to turn up and learn about OSM, that'll be cool
too :)

Music Night, on the last Wednesday of every month, starting on Wednesday
28th January. This will give interested peoples a chance to get together
and work on music projects - I'll be starting work work on my clone of
the Novation Launchpad, finally! Others are welcome to come along and
play instruments, experiment with things or something else music
related.

Questions? Comments? Date changes required?

Patches welcome.

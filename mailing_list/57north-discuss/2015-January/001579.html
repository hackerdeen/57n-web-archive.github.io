** Reminder: Call for makers
------------------------------------------------------------
Just a wee reminder that the deadline for the call for makers for the 2015 Edinburgh Mini Maker Faire is approaching soon. Get your submissions in (https://docs.google.com/forms/d/1316FkmFoLZO0NB-ypuxLA6NU7v-_j9amFeNv5AYh5M0/viewform?c=0&w=1) by Sunday 1 February if you would like to be a part of it.

The Edinburgh Mini Maker Faire returns on Sunday 19 April 2015 and it's going to be bigger and better than ever. We’re looking for people who love making things to come along to our annual celebration of creativity at Summerhall to show off their work, meet other makers and enjoy the buzz of the biggest show and tell on earth.

We've already released information on some of the makers (http://makerfaireedinburgh.com/2015/01/15/coming-up-at-the-edinburgh-mini-maker-faire-2015-2/) who will be attending this year, but there's still plenty time to get in touch and we would love to hear from you.

If you’re a maker yourself this is the perfect opportunity to share your work with a huge variety of people, generate new ideas and make new connections. Or if you know someone who makes things please suggest that they apply.

Find out more about Edinburgh Mini Maker Faire and register your interest in taking part on our website: makerfaireedinburgh.com (http://www.makerfaireedinburgh.com)

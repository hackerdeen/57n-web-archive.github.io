Hello Everyone,

An Update for the membership, On the openday last Tuesday, we were again discussing the space move, and Tom who had seen the space, put forward that we should aim to move into Kettock Lodge (https://aberdeeninnovationpark.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/03/Innovation-Park-Kettock-Lodge-Brochure-Updated-August-2019.pdf) space at a temporary location, where it would buy us time to find a new location for the space, Kettock Lodge currently occupied buy Aberdeen Action on Disability (http://aad-online.com/) we will be sharing space with them, but the space we will have is significantly larger then our current location, not in a cellar, but further away from the centre of Aberdeen.

We don't have long, so a decision should be made and we want all the member to raise any concerns.

One was members getting to the location, A few of us will be driving out due to the location, so organisation of transport by car sharing can be done, I'll probably be driving from Holburn street, and can take two people, when I am around. Others may so the same.

Any thoughts on this are welcome, but we only have a couple of weeks left, if everyone thinks this is acceptable this plan we will go ahead.

I am going to contact them an possibly arrange another viewing of the space, give me a shout if you are interested.

Midder.

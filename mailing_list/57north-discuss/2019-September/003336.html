Hi All,

***PLEASE NOTE CHANGE OF DAY AND TIME***

Sunday 15th September: #WeAreNameless - When Cyberpunk meets Nostalgia
(in the social room)

Event link: https://2600.hackers.town/wearenameless/

Thanks to Ella Kane (@ella_kane at hackers.town) for organizing this event.
#WeAreNameless is a monthly community project on the Fediverse where
Hackers and their friends watch old sci-fi cyberpunk movies.

This month's movie will be Hackers (1995) and we will begin at 22:00 UTC
(23:00 Brexit Standard Time). Please arrive with plenty of time to
spare, as we will be starting at 22:00 UTC on the dot, simulwatching
with hackers around the world.

If you are not already involved in the #Fediverse, and would like to
learn more about it, then you can get an account on our very own 57North
Hacklab instance at 57n.org. Ask me when you arrive.

Thanks,
Iain.

Yeah, no worries - I was out in Paris earlier this week at meetings, so wouldn't have been around!

We usually convene from around 7pm until the last person leaves. It's our traditional social night, so we work on projects and expect to be interrupted by conversation and comments on what everyone else is doing.  Plus there's usually some beer involved. 

At the moment we've got a few things going on - we're setting up a laser cutter, we're setting up a barcode-scanenr powered honesty bar, there will be the final preparations for the Air Quality monitoring Code the City that we're doing in partnership with the open data institute team [1] and I'm beginning to work on making some noise boxes! There's a few other personal projects happening too - I saw some wood being cut in the dirty workshop not too long ago!

Cheers,
DH
